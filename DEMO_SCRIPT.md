# AgentHub AI — Demo Video Script (3-Minute Hackathon Pitch)

This script is optimized for a **3-minute screen-recorded walkthrough** demonstrating the core features and the unique value proposition of AgentHub AI to the hackathon judges.

---

## ⏱️ Video Timeline Overview
* **0:00 - 0:30**: The Hook & Core Problem
* **0:30 - 1:15**: The AI Workforce & Supervisor Map
* **1:15 - 2:00**: Live Multi-Agent Collaboration Demo (Laptop Flow) & Explainable AI
* **2:00 - 2:40**: Company Digital Brain (RAG) & Business Simulator (X-Factor)
* **2:40 - 3:00**: Vision & Outro

---

## 🎙️ Script Details

### 🎥 Part 1: The Hook & Core Problem (0:00 - 0:30)
**Visual**: Show your face on camera or record the stunning landing page of AgentHub AI in Dark Mode. Move your cursor over the tagline: *"Hire AI Employees. Not Just AI Chatbots."*

**Audio/Voiceover**: 
> "Most companies deploying AI today are building isolated chatbots—simple FAQ bots, customer support assistants, or basic writing tools. But businesses don't run on isolated tasks; they run on workflows. 
> 
> Welcome to **AgentHub AI**—the Operating System for AI Employees. Instead of a single chatbot, AgentHub allows you to deploy a fully coordinated, multi-department AI workforce that collaborates automatically to solve complex business goals."

---

### 🎥 Part 2: The AI Workforce & Map (0:30 - 1:15)
**Visual**: Switch to the **Workforce Dashboard**. Hover over the KPI metrics (Saved Hours, Revenue Assisted, Hired Agents). Scroll down to show the **Interactive Agent Map** (the supervisor connecting to Sales, HR, Finance, Analytics nodes).

**Audio/Voiceover**:
> "Here is our main dashboard. We currently have seven active AI employees hired across different departments, including Sales, Support, HR, Finance, and Analytics, all coordinated by our autonomous **Supervisor Agent**.
> 
> If you need to expand your team, you can visit the **Agent Marketplace** to install specialized employees like a Legal Agent or a Developer Agent with a single click. Every employee has custom memory, tools, and designated permissions."

---

### 🎥 Part 3: Live Multi-Agent Collaboration (1:15 - 2:00)
**Visual**: Type or select the demo query: *"I need 250 laptops for my company"* and click **Run Query**. 
As the animation runs:
1. Show the supervisor node pulse blue.
2. Show the connection lines light up and the **Sales Agent** node pulse.
3. Show the **Finance Agent** node pulse.
4. Show the **Meeting Agent** and **Analytics Agent** pulse.
5. Point at the real-time log updates as they stream in.
6. Click on a log to open the **Explainable AI** side-drawer.

**Audio/Voiceover**:
> "Let’s see them in action. Imagine a customer sends an email saying: *'I need 250 laptops for my company.'* 
> 
> Watch how the workflow executes. The **Supervisor Agent** receives the task, reads our pricing catalog, and routes it to the **Sales Agent**. Sales qualifies the lead, applies a volume discount, and passes the quote to the **Finance Agent**, who drafts an official invoice. Simultaneously, the **Meeting Agent** schedules a follow-up and the **Analytics Agent** forecasts an 82% closing probability.
> 
> With **Explainable AI**, you can click any trace log to see the exact reasoning, confidence score, and the document sources used to make the decision."

---

### 🎥 Part 4: Digital Brain & Business Simulator (2:00 - 2:40)
**Visual**: Click on the **Company Brain (RAG)** tab. Show the uploaded catalog and policies, and the force-directed connections in the **Company Knowledge Graph**. 
Then, switch to the **Business Simulator** tab. Drag the price adjustment slider to `+15%` and watch the volume, revenue, profit, and churn metrics recalculate instantly with animated bars.

**Audio/Voiceover**:
> "All of this is backed by our **Company Digital Brain**. Any uploaded catalog, contract, or email is parsed, vectorized, and indexed into a searchable knowledge graph.
> 
> We’ve also built a **Business Simulator**. What happens if we increase base prices by 15%? By adjusting this slider, our built-in elasticity model forecasts the impact on sales volume, profit margins, and churn risk, giving executives data-driven recommendations in seconds."

---

### 🎥 Part 5: Outro (2:40 - 3:00)
**Visual**: Return to the main dashboard showing the updated KPI counters (revenue increased, hours saved incremented). Show landing page again.

**Audio/Voiceover**:
> "By turning individual AI models into collaborative teammates, AgentHub AI has already saved our workspace over 150 manual hours and managed thousands of dollars in pipeline deals. 
> 
> This isn't just automation—it's the future of the autonomous enterprise. Thank you."
